1. Fundamentals of ERP and MES Systems  (30%)
   - Definition and scope of ERP and MES  
   - Core components of ERP (finance, HR, supply chain, production)  
   - Core components of MES (production planning, execution, quality, maintenance)  
   - Differences between ERP and MES in function and data flow  
   - Integration architecture: ERP-MES interface protocols (MES-ERP data exchange)  

2. Manufacturing Process Modeling and Simulation (30%)
   - Flowcharting and process mapping of manufacturing operations  
   - Simulation tools for production line design (AnyLogic, FlexSim)  
   - Validation of process models against real-world data  
   - Role of simulation in identifying bottlenecks and optimizing throughput  

3. Real-Time Production Monitoring and Control  
   - Sensors, IoT integration, and real-time data acquisition  
   - MES functions in monitoring machine status, OEE, and downtime  
   - Real-time alerts and corrective actions  

4. Quality Management in MES  
   - In-line quality inspection and defect tracking  
   - Integration with quality standards (ISO 9001, Six Sigma)  
   - Root cause analysis and corrective action loops  

5. Maintenance and Predictive Analytics  
   - Preventive and predictive maintenance workflows  
   - Condition monitoring and failure prediction using MES  
   - Integration with CMMS (Computerized Maintenance Management Systems)  

6. Sustainable Manufacturing and Circular Design  
   - Lifecycle assessment in production planning  
   - Material efficiency, waste reduction, and energy use tracking  
   - Sustainable design principles in MES/ERP workflows   

7. Human-Machine Interaction and Decision Support  
   - User interface design in ERP/MES dashboards  
   - Decision support tools for operators and managers  
   - Training modules for system usability  

8. Case Studies in Metal Industry Applications  
   - Real-world implementation of ERP/MES in metal fabrication  
   - Challenges in scalability and data interoperability  
   - Lessons learned from simulation-based design
