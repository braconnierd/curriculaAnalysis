1. System Design Fundamentals (20%)
   - Definition and scope of manufacturing systems  
   - Components of a manufacturing system (machines, materials, information, people)  
   - System boundaries and interfaces  
   - Design objectives: efficiency, flexibility, sustainability  

2. Product-Process Integration  
   - Design for manufacturing (DFM)  
   - Design for assembly (DFA)  
   - Product lifecycle integration  
   - Interfacing product design with manufacturing processes 

3. Flexibility and Scalability (20%)
   - Modular design principles 
   - Standardized interfaces (hardware and software) 
   - Changeable systems and reconfigurability  
   - Granularity and complexity in system design 
   - Scalable architectures for varying production volumes  

4. Sustainable Manufacturing Practices  
   - Environmental impact assessment in design  
   - Energy efficiency
   - Material utilization
   - Waste reduction  
   - Circular economy principles in system design

5. Additive Manufacturing Integration  
   - Applications in prototyping and production  
   - Design considerations for 3D printing (material, geometry, support)  
   - Integration with traditional manufacturing systems  

6. Simulation and Modeling  
   - Use of simulation tools in metal and manufacturing systems  
   - Dynamic modeling of production flows  
   - Validation and optimization of system performance

7. Maintenance and Lifecycle Management (20%)  
   - Predictive and preventive maintenance planning  
   - Maintenance strategies in learning factory environments 
   - Integration of maintenance into system design  

8. Human-Centered and Learning Factory Design  
   - Role of human operators in system performance  
   - Learning-by-doing environments  
   - Skill development and hands-on training 

9. Digital Twin and Real-Time Monitoring  
   - Concept, fidelity and implementation of digital twins  
   - Real-time data acquisition and feedback loops  
   - Use in monitoring and optimizing manufacturing systems  

10. Case Studies in Real-World Systems  
   - Analysis of industrial systems using granular complexity models  
   - Metal industry applications and performance benchmarks
