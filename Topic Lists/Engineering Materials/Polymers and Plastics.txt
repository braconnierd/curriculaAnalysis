1. Fundamental Polymer Chemistry  (20%)
    - Structure of polymer chains (linear, branched, cross-linked)  
    - Monomer types and polymerization mechanisms (addition, condensation)  
    - Polymerization kinetics and reaction conditions  
    - Molecular weight distribution and its impact on properties  
    - Chain end effects and stereochemistry  

2. Polymer Classification and Properties (20%) 
    - Thermoplastics vs. thermosets vs. elastomers  
    - Glass transition temperature (Tg) and melting point (Tm)  
    - Mechanical, thermal, and optical properties  
    - Density, crystallinity, and morphology  

3. Polymer Processing Techniques  (20%)
    - Extrusion, injection molding, blow molding, thermoforming  
    - Die design, flow behavior, and processing parameters  
    - Cooling and solidification dynamics  
    - Defects and quality control in processing  

4. Performance and Applications  
    - Selection of polymers for specific applications (packaging, automotive)  
    - Environmental and functional requirements  
    - Case studies in real-world product design  

5. Safety, Health, and Environmental Impact  
    - Hazardous materials in polymer production  
    - Emissions and waste management  
    - Regulatory standards (RoHS, REACH)  

6. Plastic Recycling and Sustainability  
    - Types of plastic waste and contamination  
    - Mechanical vs. chemical recycling  
    - Feedstock degradation and reprocessing challenges  
    - Life cycle analysis of polymer products
