1. Foundational Materials Science  (30%)
    - Structure and properties of polymers, metals, and ceramics  
    - Molecular structure and chain orientation in polymers  
    - Phase transitions and crystallinity  
    - Mechanical behavior under load (tensile, compressive, shear)  
    - Failure mechanisms in traditional and composite materials  

2. Composite Material Design Principles (30%)
    - Matrix and reinforcement selection (glass, carbon, aramid)  
    - Fiber alignment and orientation effects  
    - Interfacial bonding and adhesion mechanisms  
    - Design for load transfer and stress distribution  
    - Selection criteria based on application (aerospace, automotive, biomedical)  

3. Manufacturing Processes for Composites  
    - Hand lay-up and vacuum-assisted resin transfer molding (VARTM)  
    - Compression molding and injection molding  
    - Automated fiber placement (AFP) and tape laying  
    - Process parameters: temperature, pressure, time, resin viscosity  
    - Quality control and defect detection (voids, delamination)  

4. Mechanical and Environmental Performance  
    - Strength, stiffness, and durability under dynamic loading  
    - Fatigue and creep behavior  
    - Environmental degradation (moisture, UV, temperature)  
    - Long-term performance in real-world conditions  

5. Testing and Evaluation Methods  
    - Tensile, compressive, and shear testing  
    - Impact and fracture testing  
    - Non-destructive evaluation (NDT): ultrasonic, X-ray, thermography  
    - Data analysis and failure mode identification  

6. Lifecycle and Sustainability  
    - Material sourcing and recyclability  
    - End-of-life considerations and circular design  
    - Environmental impact of manufacturing and disposal  
    - Life cycle assessment (LCA) basics  

7. Skill Development and Industry Alignment  
    - Hands-on lab work with composite materials  
    - Use of simulation software (ANSYS, Abaqus)  
    - Problem-solving and design thinking in real-world scenarios  
    - Integration of video-based tutorials for process visualization
