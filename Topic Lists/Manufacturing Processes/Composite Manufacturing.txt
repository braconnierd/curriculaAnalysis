1. Fundamentals of Composite Materials (30%)
   - Molecular structure and bonding in composites  
   - Classification: fiber-reinforced, polymer-matrix, ceramic-matrix  
   - Properties: strength, stiffness, thermal stability, fatigue behavior  
   - Failure mechanisms and fracture analysis  

2. Composite Manufacturing Processes  
   - Hand lay-up and vacuum bagging  
   - Resin transfer molding (RTM)  
   - Compression molding  
   - Autoclave curing  
   - Pultrusion and filament winding  
   - Process parameters: temperature, pressure, time, resin content  

3. Design for Manufacturing (DfM) in Composites (30%)
   - Geometric considerations: part geometry, draft angles, wall thickness  
   - Material selection based on performance and cost  
   - Integration of DfM with CAD/CAM tools  
   - Tolerance and assembly design  

4. Additive Manufacturing of Composites  
   - 3D printing of polymer matrix composites (PMCs)  
   - Layer-by-layer deposition and fiber alignment  
   - Post-processing: curing, surface finishing  
   - Challenges: warping, porosity, interfacial bonding  

5. Simulation and Visualization Tools  
   - Process simulation: thermal, mechanical, flow  
   - Real-time visualization of curing and layer formation  
   - Use of CAE software (ANSYS, Abaqus, COMSOL)  

6. Sustainable and Green Manufacturing  
   - Life cycle assessment (LCA) of composite systems  
   - Recycling and end-of-life strategies  
   - Energy efficiency and emissions reduction
