1. Foundations of Digital Twin (20%)
   - Definition and evolution from Industry 4.0 to Industry 5.0  
   - Core components: physical object, virtual replica, data exchange, real-time feedback  
   - Lifecycle alignment with product and manufacturing phases

2. Digital Twin in Product Design and Development (20%) 
   - Design-to-manufacturing integration via digital twin  
   - Real-time simulation of design performance  
   - Feedback loops for iterative design optimization 

3. Digital Twin and Manufacturing Process Integration (20%)
   - Linking digital twin to CAD, CAE, CAM  
   - Process simulation and validation  
   - Error detection and predictive maintenance

4. Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) and Sustainability in Digital Twins  
   - Embedding environmental impact analysis in digital twin models  
   - Real-time monitoring of carbon footprint and resource use  
   - Sustainable design decisions via digital twin feedback  

5. Learning-by-Doing and Hands-On Digital Twin Experiences
   - Lab-based implementation of digital twin prototypes  
   - Use of simulation tools (ANSYS, Siemens NX)  
   - Student-led design projects with real-time data feedback 

6. Design for Additive Manufacturing (DFAM) in Digital Twins  
   - Topology optimization and material behavior simulation  
   - Integration of DFAM constraints into digital twin models

7. Digital Twin and Geometric Dimensioning & Tolerancing (GD&T)  
   - Precision modeling and tolerance analysis in virtual environments  
   - Real-time validation of design fit and manufacturability

8. Industry 5.0 and Human-Centric Digital Twin Applications  
   - Role of human-in-the-loop decision-making in digital twin systems  
   - Collaboration between engineers, operators, and end-users  
   - Human-machine interaction in real-time adaptive systems

9. Cross-Disciplinary Integration  
   - Value creation through digital twin-driven innovation  
   - New product development using digital twin-enabled design and testing  
   - Entrepreneurial thinking in product lifecycle management
