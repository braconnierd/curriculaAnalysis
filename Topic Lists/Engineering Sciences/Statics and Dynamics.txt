1. Particle Equilibrium and Free-Body Diagrams (15%) 
   - Types of forces (gravitational, normal, friction, tension)  
   - Vector resolution and components  
   - Static equilibrium conditions (ΣF = 0, ΣM = 0)  
   - Free-body diagram construction and validation  

2. Rigid Body Kinematics (15%) 
   - Translation, rotation, and general motion  
   - Angular displacement, velocity, and acceleration  
   - Rotational kinematics equations  
   - Relative motion analysis  

3. Rigid Body Dynamics (15%) 
   - Newton’s second law for rigid bodies  
   - Moment of inertia and mass moment of inertia  
   - Rotational kinetic energy  
   - Work-energy principle  
   - Impulse and momentum  

4. Planar Motion and Center of Mass  (15%) 
   - Center of mass and centroid  
   - Motion of systems of particles  
   - Moment of inertia of composite bodies  

5. Mechanical Systems and Constraints  
   - Degrees of freedom and constraint equations  
   - Idealized mechanical systems (pulleys, gears, linkages)  
   - Virtual work and Lagrange’s equations (introductory)  

6. Dynamic Analysis of Machines  
   - Vibration fundamentals (free, damped, forced)  
   - Natural frequency and damping ratio  
   - Response to harmonic loads  

7. Design for Manufacturability and Simulation  
   - CAD/CAE integration in design  
   - Simulation of static and dynamic loads  
   - Failure analysis and stress visualization
