1. Fundamentals of Material Forming (20%)
   - Deformation mechanisms (plasticity, strain hardening)  
   - Stress-strain relationships  
   - Flow curves and yield criteria  
   - Material behavior under load (ductility, brittleness)  

2. Bulk Forming Processes (20%)
   - Rolling (hot, cold, continuous)  
   - Extrusion (forward, backward, hydrostatic)  
   - Forging (open-die, closed-die, impression die)  
   - Press working (bending, pressing)  
   - Process parameters and tool design  

3. Sheet Metal Forming Processes (20%)
   - Rolling (sheet production)  
   - Drawing (deep drawing, progressive)  
   - Bending (press bending, roll bending)  
   - Blanking and punching  
   - Formability limits and springback  

4. Process Selection and Optimization  
   - Criteria for process selection (cost, efficiency, material, geometry)  
   - Trade-offs between process types  
   - Decision models (process selection model)  

5. Design for Manufacturing (DFM)  
   - Geometric considerations (draft angles, wall thickness)  
   - Tolerancing and dimensional accuracy  
   - Material selection for forming  

6. Simulation and Visualization Tools  
   - Finite element analysis (FEA) of forming processes  
   - Real-time process visualization  
   - Use of software (DEFORM, ANSYS)  

7. Failure Analysis and Mitigation  
   - Crack propagation, necking, void formation  
   - Root cause analysis in forming defects  
   - Quality control and inspection techniques  

8. Sustainable and Green Manufacturing  
   - Energy efficiency in forming  
   - Waste reduction strategies  
   - Lifecycle assessment of materials  

9. Additive Manufacturing in Forming Contexts  
   - Layer-by-layer material deposition  
   - Sintering and fusion processes  
   - Post-processing for mechanical integrity  
   - Integration with traditional forming
