1. Maintenance Strategy Selection (20%)
   - Preventive vs. Predictive vs. Reactive Maintenance  
   - Decision frameworks for strategy selection based on asset criticality and cost  
   - Lifecycle cost analysis in strategy evaluation  

2. Maintenance Planning and Scheduling (20%)
   - Work order generation and prioritization  
   - Time-based vs. condition-based scheduling  
   - Integration with production planning systems  

3. Condition Monitoring and Diagnostics
   - Vibration, thermal, and acoustic monitoring  
   - Signal processing and anomaly detection  
   - Use of sensors and IoT in real-time monitoring  

4. Failure Mode and Effects Analysis (FMEA) 
   - Failure mode identification and impact assessment  
   - Risk prioritization and mitigation strategies  
   - Integration with product lifecycle design  

5. Maintenance System Design and Integration (20%)
   - Modular and scalable maintenance architectures  
   - Interfacing with manufacturing systems (MES, PLCs)  
   - Digital twin applications in maintenance planning  

6. Sustainability in Maintenance 
   - Environmental impact of maintenance activities  
   - Energy efficiency and waste reduction in maintenance operations  
   - Lifecycle thinking in maintenance decisions  

7. Additive Manufacturing in Maintenance 
   - On-demand spare part fabrication  
   - Rapid prototyping for repair and replacement  
   - Material selection and quality assurance  

8. Simulation-Based Maintenance System Development 
   - Dynamic modeling of maintenance workflows  
   - Scenario testing under varying operational conditions  
   - Granularity complexity in system design and response  

9. Human Factors and Training in Maintenance 
   - Skill development and competency mapping  
   - Safety protocols and procedural compliance  
   - Learning factory environments for hands-on training  

10. Performance Metrics and KPIs
   - MTBF, MTTR, availability, and downtime analysis  
   - Benchmarking and continuous improvement cycles
