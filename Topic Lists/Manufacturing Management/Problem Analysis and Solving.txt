1. Problem Identification and Framing (20%)
   - Defining manufacturing problems using real-world scenarios  
   - Identifying root causes via 5 Whys and Fishbone diagrams  
   - Translating vague issues into measurable objectives  

2. Data Collection and Measurement Systems (20%) 
   - Designing measurement protocols for physical dimensions and process variables  
   - Selecting appropriate tools (rulers, calipers, protractors) and units (mm, degrees)  
   - Establishing consistent sampling methods (fixed-number, systematic sampling)  

3. Statistical Process Control (SPC)  
   - Constructing X-bar and R charts using Excel  
   - Interpreting control limits and detecting out-of-control points  
   - Distinguishing random vs. non-random variation  

4. Process Design and Flowcharting (20%) 
   - Developing step-by-step production workflows  
   - Creating flowcharts with task sequences and work assignments  
   - Designing jig/fixture integration for repeatability  

5. Quality Management Principles  
   - Applying ISO 9001 and PDCA cycles  
   - Defining quality metrics (dimensional accuracy, defect rates)  
   - Evaluating process capability (Cp, Cpk)  

6. Supply Chain Impact on Financial Outcomes  
   - Linking process inefficiencies to cost overruns and revenue loss  
   - Analyzing lead time, inventory, and defect costs  

7. Iterative Improvement and Continuous Quality Enhancement  
   - Applying Lean principles (5S, Kaizen)  
   - Designing feedback loops for process refinement  

8. Experiential Learning and Hands-On Simulation  
   - Using game-based simulations (business games) to model decision-making  
   - Implementing SAP-based logistics scenarios for real-time feedback  

9. Ethical Decision-Making in Manufacturing  
   - Assessing trade-offs between cost, quality, and environmental impact  
   - Evaluating compliance with safety and sustainability standards  

10. Project Management in Manufacturing 
   - Planning timelines, resource allocation, and risk mitigation  
   - Applying project management tools (Gantt charts, milestones)  

11. Systematic Innovation and Patent Analysis  
   - Using patent databases to identify emerging technologies  
   - Evaluating innovation pathways for future manufacturing  

12. Collaborative Learning and Industry Integration 
   - Facilitating team-based problem solving with industry partners  
   - Using learning factories for real-world process exposure
