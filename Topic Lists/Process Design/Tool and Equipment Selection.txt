1. Tool and Equipment Selection Fundamentals (20%) 
   - Definition and scope of tool/equipment selection  
   - Role in manufacturing process efficiency  
   - Decision-making frameworks (cost-benefit, lifecycle, performance)  
   - Key performance indicators (KPIs) for tool selection  

2. Material Properties and Tool Compatibility  (20%)
   - Mechanical properties (strength, hardness, ductility)  
   - Thermal and wear behavior of materials  
   - Matching tool materials to workpiece materials  
   - Effect of material microstructure on tool life  

3. Process Requirements and Tool Functionality (20%) 
   - Cutting, forming, joining, and assembly processes  
   - Tool geometry and its impact on process outcomes  
   - Tool stiffness, alignment, and vibration control  
   - Tool integration with process flow  

4. Sustainability and Environmental Impact  
   - Tool lifecycle analysis (acquisition, use, disposal)  
   - Energy consumption and emissions during operation  
   - Reusability, recyclability, and material sourcing  
   - Alignment with Industry 4.0 and Industry 5.0 sustainability goals  

5. Human Factors and Ergonomics  
   - Operator interaction with tools  
   - Safety and risk assessment in tool use  
   - Training needs and skill development  
   - Cognitive load and decision support  

7. Cost, Maintenance, and Availability  
   - Capital and operational cost analysis  
   - Maintenance schedules and failure rates  
   - Tool availability and supply chain considerations  
   - Total cost of ownership (TCO) modeling  

9. Evaluation and Feedback Loops  
   - Performance metrics post-deployment  
   - Data collection and analysis for improvement  
   - Iterative refinement of selection models
