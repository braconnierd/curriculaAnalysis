1. Leadership Foundations (20%) 
   - Core leadership theories (transformational, situational, servant)  
   - Leadership styles and their application in project environments  
   - Emotional intelligence and decision-making under uncertainty  
   - Ethical leadership and integrity in team dynamics  

2. Project Management Frameworks  
   - PMBOK, PRINCE2, and Agile project structures  
   - Project lifecycle phases (initiation, planning, execution, monitoring, closure)  
   - Scope, time, cost, quality, and risk management  
   - Stakeholder analysis and communication planning  

3. Team Dynamics and Collaboration (20%)  
   - Team formation and roles (facilitator, leader, contributor)  
   - Conflict resolution and group decision-making  
   - Interpersonal communication and active listening  
   - Building trust and psychological safety  

4. Project Planning and Design (20%)
   - Work breakdown structures (WBS) and task decomposition  
   - Gantt charts, critical path method (CPM), and scheduling  
   - Resource allocation and capacity planning  
   - Risk identification and mitigation strategies  

5. Quality Management in Projects  
   - Quality planning, assurance, and control  
   - ISO 9001 principles and quality metrics  
   - Statistical process control (SPC) and process capability  
   - Root cause analysis and continuous improvement (Kaizen)  

6. Ethical Decision-Making in Leadership  
   - Case-based ethical dilemmas in project execution  
   - Professional responsibility and accountability  
   - Balancing innovation with compliance  

7. Experiential Learning and Simulation  
   - Designing hands-on project simulations (manufacturing, logistics)  
   - Using business simulation games to teach leadership and project execution  
   - Reflective practice and post-experience evaluation  

8. Integration of Technology and Tools  
   - Digital tools for project tracking (MS Project, Trello)  
   - Use of AI and data analytics in project monitoring  
   - Systematic patent analysis for innovation assessment  

9. Evaluation and Continuous Improvement  
   - Performance metrics and KPIs for project success  
   - Feedback loops and iterative refinement  
   - Post-project review and lessons learned  

10. Interdisciplinary Project Execution  
   - Cross-functional collaboration in engineering and supply chain contexts  
   - Integration of supply chain operations into project planning  
   - Real-world case studies from manufacturing and logistics
