1. Introduction to Operations Research (OR) (20%) 
   - Definition and scope of OR  
   - Historical development and key figures  
   - Applications in supply chain, manufacturing, and logistics  
   - OR vs. optimization vs. decision science  
   - Problem formulation: objectives, constraints, variables  
   - Case study: OR in airline scheduling

2. Mathematical Modeling and Optimization (20%)  
   - Linear programming (LP) and its applications  
   - Integer programming (IP) and mixed-integer programming (MIP)  
   - Nonlinear programming (NLP)  
   - Goal programming and multi-objective optimization  
   - Sensitivity analysis and duality  
   - Software tools: Excel Solver, Gurobi, Python (SciPy)  

3. Forecasting Methods (20%)  
   - Time series decomposition (trend, seasonality, cycle)  
   - Classical methods: moving averages, exponential smoothing  
   - Advanced methods: ARIMA, SARIMA, Holt-Winters  
   - Regression-based forecasting  
   - Model validation and error metrics (MAE, RMSE)  
   - Forecasting in supply chain context  

4. Statistical Process Control (SPC)  
   - Control charts: X-bar, R, p-charts  
   - Process capability (Cp, Cpk)  
   - Sources of variation: random vs. non-random  
   - Design of experiments (DOE) basics  
   - Hands-on SPC exercise: “Manufacturing Squares”  

5. Decision Analysis and Risk Modeling  
   - Decision trees and utility theory  
   - Expected value and risk assessment  
   - Scenario analysis and Monte Carlo simulation  
   - Application in supply chain risk management  

6. Ethics in Operations Research and Forecasting  
   - Bias in data and model assumptions  
   - Transparency and accountability in forecasting  
   - Ethical implications of automation and AI in logistics  

7. Experiential Learning and Simulation  
   - Business simulation games (supply chain, manufacturing)  
   - Learning factory environments   
   - SAP-based logistics simulations  

8. Quality Management and Continuous Improvement  
   - PDCA cycle and Kaizen  
   - Root cause analysis (5 Whys, Fishbone)  
   - Quality function deployment (QFD)  
   - Competency frameworks for engineering graduates   

9. Integration of OR and Forecasting in Supply Chains  
   - Demand forecasting and inventory optimization  
   - Production planning and scheduling  
   - Real-time decision systems  

10. Capstone Project: End-to-End Supply Chain Simulation  
   - Design, model, forecast, and optimize a supply chain  
   - Use of SPC and OR tools  
   - Present findings with control charts and sensitivity analysis
