1. Fundamentals of Metrology  
   - Definition and scope of metrology  
   - Units and systems (SI, imperial)  
   - Traceability and calibration standards  
   - Measurement uncertainty and error types (systematic, random)  
   - Accuracy, precision, resolution, repeatability, reproducibility  

2. Instrumentation Principles  
   - Types of measuring instruments (rulers, calipers, protractors, digital tools)  
   - Mechanical vs. electronic instruments  
   - Sensitivity, range, and resolution  
   - Instrument calibration and verification procedures  

3. Measurement Techniques and Tools  
   - Linear measurement (length, width, edge alignment)  
   - Angular measurement (diagonals, angles)  
   - Surface finish and edge quality assessment  
   - Use of jigs and templates in alignment and consistency  

4. Error Analysis and Uncertainty Propagation  
   - Sources of measurement error (tool wear, human error, environmental)  
   - Error propagation in multi-step measurements  
   - Mitigation strategies for variation  

5. Statistical Process Control (SPC)  
   - X-bar and R charts construction and interpretation  
   - Control limits (UCL, LCL) calculation  
   - Process stability and capability (Cp, Cpk)  
   - Detection of random vs. non-random variation  
   - Use of Excel-based templates for real-time monitoring  

6. Quality Management and Process Design  
   - Quality standards (ISO 9001, design specifications)  
   - Specification limits vs. tolerance bands  
   - Process capability analysis (Cp, Cpk)  
   - Design of experiments (DoE) for process optimization  

7. Application in Manufacturing Processes  
   - Hands-on production trials (“Manufacturing Squares”)  
   - Flowchart design for production and inspection  
   - Sampling strategies (systematic sampling)  
   - Iterative improvement via PDCA cycle  

8. Process Improvement and Feedback Loops  
   - Root cause analysis (5 Whys, fishbone)  
   - Implementation of corrective actions  
   - Recommendations for full-scale production  

9. Integration of Quality and Engineering Practice  
   - Role of quality in engineering design and decision-making  
   - Professional competency in quality assurance (Venig)  
   - Vertical integration of quality concepts across engineering courses (Ker)
