1. Generative Design Fundamentals (20%)
   - Definition and evolution of generative design  
   - Core principles: optimization, constraints, parameters, and feedback loops  
   - Comparison with traditional design methods  
   - Role of algorithms and AI in generative design  

2. Algorithmic Thinking and Programming for Design (20%)
   - Basic programming in design environments (Grasshopper, MATLAB)  
   - Parametric modeling and scripting  
   - Input-output logic in generative systems  
   - Debugging and iterative refinement  

3. Digital Twin and Simulation in Generative Design 
   - Real-time simulation of design performance  
   - Integration of digital twins with generative feedback  
   - Use of simulation to validate design outputs  

4. Sustainability and Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) Integration (20%) 
   - Environmental impact assessment in generative design  
   - Embedding LCA metrics into design parameters  
   - Optimization for material efficiency and carbon footprint  

5. Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing (GD&T) in Generative Outputs  
   - GD&T as a constraint in generative design  
   - Validation of generative outputs against tolerancing standards  
   - Interdisciplinary integration with manufacturing feasibility  

6. Manufacturing Process Integration  
   - Compatibility of generative outputs with CNC, CAM, and additive manufacturing  
   - Process feasibility analysis and tooling constraints  
   - Case studies in real-world manufacturing workflows  

7. Human-Computer Interaction and User-Centered Design  
   - Designing interfaces for generative design tools  
   - User feedback loops and iterative design  
   - Role of gamification in design exploration  

8. Evaluation and Validation of Generative Design Outcomes   
   - Metrics for performance, durability, and manufacturability  
   - Comparative analysis with hand-designed alternatives  
   - Peer review and experiential assessment
