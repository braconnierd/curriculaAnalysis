1. Foundations of Quality Management  
   - Definition and history of quality (Deming, Juran, Crosby)  
   - Quality philosophy: conformance, fitness for use, customer satisfaction  
   - Core principles of Total Quality Management (TQM)  

2. Quality Standards and Frameworks  
   - ISO 9001:2015 structure and implementation  
   - Quality management system (QMS) lifecycle: planning, operation, monitoring, improvement  
   - Key elements: documentation, leadership, risk-based thinking  

3. Statistical Process Control (SPC)  
   - X-bar and R charts: construction, interpretation, control limits  
   - Process stability and capability (Cp, Cpk)  
   - Types of variation: common vs. special causes  
   - Sampling plans: AQL, sampling frequency, sample size selection  

4. Design for Quality (DFQ) and Quality Function Deployment (QFD)  
   - QFD: House of Quality, customer requirements translation  
   - Design inputs and outputs for quality integration  
   - Early integration of quality into product and process design  

5. Root Cause Analysis and Problem Solving  
   - Fishbone (Ishikawa) diagrams  
   - 5 Whys and failure mode and effects analysis (FMEA)  
   - Data collection and analysis techniques  

6. Lean Principles and Continuous Improvement  
   - Lean tools: value stream mapping, 5S, Kaizen  
   - PDCA cycle: Plan-Do-Check-Act in quality contexts  
   - Integration of lean with SPC and QMS  

7. Process Capability and Performance Metrics  
   - Measurement of process performance (Pp, Ppk)  
   - Process capability indices and their interpretation  
   - Benchmarking and performance tracking  

8. Implementation and Management of QMS  
   - Roles and responsibilities in QMS  
   - Internal audits and compliance  
   - Corrective and preventive actions (CAPA)  

9. Case Studies and Practical Applications  
   - Real-world manufacturing scenarios (“Manufacturing Squares” exercise)  
   - Hands-on SPC trials with physical products  
   - Iterative improvement through experimentation  

10. Technology Integration in Quality Management  
   - Use of Excel and digital tools for SPC and data analysis  
   - Automation and AI in quality monitoring (ChatGPT in QFD)
