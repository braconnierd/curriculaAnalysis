1. Foundations of Joining  (20%)
   - Welding (GMAW, TIG, MIG, SMAW)  
   - Fastening (bolts, rivets, adhesives)  
   - Joining materials compatibility  
   - Joint design principles (bevels, fillets)  
   - Defects and inspection (porosity, cracks)  
   - Safety protocols in joining operations  

2. Fabrication Processes  (20%)
   - Cutting (laser, plasma, waterjet)  
   - Forming (bending, rolling, pressing)  
   - Machining (turning, milling, grinding)  
   - Sheet metal fabrication (cutting, bending, assembly)  
   - Tool selection and tool wear  
   - Process planning and workflow design  

3. Additive Manufacturing (AM)  
   - AM technologies (FDM, SLS, SLA, binder jetting)  
   - Material selection for AM  
   - Layer-by-layer process mechanics  
   - Post-processing (sintering, polishing, heat treatment)  
   - AM design for manufacturability (DFM)  
   - AM quality control and defect analysis  

4. Finishing Techniques  (20%)
   - Surface finishing (grinding, polishing, sandblasting)  
   - Coating and plating (electroplating, PVD, CVD)  
   - Surface treatment (anodizing, heat treatment)  
   - Dimensional and surface tolerance control  
   - Finishing equipment operation and maintenance  

5. Design for Manufacturing (DFM)  
   - DFM principles in joining, fabrication, and finishing  
   - Process integration and workflow optimization  
   - Material selection based on process constraints  
   - Cost-benefit analysis of fabrication methods  

6. Sustainable and Green Manufacturing  
   - Energy efficiency in processes  
   - Waste reduction in joining and fabrication  
   - Lifecycle assessment of materials and processes  
   - Environmental impact of finishing techniques
