1. Foundations of Digital Twin (18%)
   - Definition and evolution of digital twin concept  
   - Core components: physical object, virtual model, data flow, real-time synchronization  
   - Lifecycle stages: design, operation, maintenance, decommissioning  

2. Mathematical and Analytical Foundations (18%)
   - Differential equations and system dynamics  
   - Optimization techniques in engineering design  
   - Sensitivity analysis and uncertainty modeling  
   - Application to additive manufacturing design  

3. Real-Time Data Acquisition and Sensor Integration (18%)
   - Sensor types and placement in manufacturing systems  
   - Data streams and signal processing  
   - IoT integration and edge computing in real-time monitoring
   - Interoperability standards: OPC UA, MQTT, and industrial protocols

4. Virtual Simulation and Modeling  (18%)
   - 3D modeling and CAD integration  
   - Simulation/visualization of manufacturing processes (CAM programming)  
   - Serious game-based simulation for training
   - Virtual reality (VR) for immersive learning and sustainability assessment 

5. Feedback Loops and Adaptive Control  
   - Closed-loop control systems in digital twin environments  
   - Machine learning for predictive maintenance  
   - Real-time decision-making and anomaly detection  

6. Human-Centric Design and Sustainability 
   - User interface design for digital twin systems  
   - Human-in-the-loop decision making  
   - Sustainable manufacturing metrics and lifecycle analysis
