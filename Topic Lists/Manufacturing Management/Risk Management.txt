1. Risk Identification and Assessment (20%)  
   - Sources of risk in engineering and supply chain systems  
   - Hazard identification using checklists and failure mode analysis (FMEA)  
   - Risk categorization: operational, financial, technical, environmental  
   - Quantitative vs. qualitative risk assessment methods  

2. Risk Analysis Tools and Techniques (20%)  
   - Failure Mode and Effects Analysis (FMEA)  
   - Fault Tree Analysis (FTA)  
   - Monte Carlo simulation for probabilistic risk modeling  
   - SWOT analysis in supply chain context  

3. Risk Mitigation and Response Planning (20%) 
   - Contingency planning and emergency response protocols  
   - Redundancy design and backup systems  
   - Risk transfer mechanisms (insurance, outsourcing)  
   - Business continuity planning (BCP)  

4. Risk Communication and Stakeholder Engagement  
   - Transparent reporting of risk to management and public  
   - Stakeholder risk perception and feedback loops  
   - Risk communication in supply chain operations  

5. Ethical and Regulatory Compliance in Risk Management  
   - Legal and regulatory frameworks (ISO 31000, OSHA)  
   - Ethical dilemmas in risk decision-making  
   - Responsibility of engineering professionals in risk governance  

6. Integration of Risk Management in Design and Operations  
   - Risk-informed design processes  
   - Embedding risk assessment in product and process development  
   - Risk management in lean and agile manufacturing  
   - Cross-functional risk coordination (design, operations, logistics)  
   - Integration with quality management and supply chain resilience  

7. Real-Time Monitoring and Decision Support Systems  
   - Use of sensors and IoT for real-time risk detection  
   - Digital twins for predictive risk modeling  
   - Data analytics for early warning systems  

8. Case-Based and Experiential Learning in Risk Management  
   - Simulated risk scenarios in supply chain and manufacturing  
   - Business simulation games for risk response training  
   - Hands-on risk experiments using learning factories  
   - Experiential learning projects using systematic patent analysis to enhance awareness of patent-based intellectual property rights (IPR)  
   - Case-based undergraduate operations research course implementation  
   - Integrative statistical process control exercises (Manufacturing Squares)  

9. Evaluation and Continuous Improvement of Risk Processes 
   - Post-event review and root cause analysis  
   - KPIs for risk performance  
   - Iterative refinement of risk management plans  

10. Interdisciplinary Risk Management in Engineering Systems  
   - Cross-functional risk coordination (design, operations, logistics)  
   - Integration with quality management and supply chain resilience  
   - Synergies between risk management and lean manufacturing principles  
   - Application of risk-informed design in intelligent and sustainable engineering systems
