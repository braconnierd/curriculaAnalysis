1. Fundamentals of Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS) (20%) 
   - Definition and evolution of CPS  
   - Core components: physical systems, cyber components, integration layers  
   - Architecture of CPS: sensing, actuation, communication, control  
   - Real-world examples: smart manufacturing, autonomous vehicles, smart grids  

2. Embedded Systems and Real-Time Control (20%) 
   - Microcontroller architecture and programming (C, C++)  
   - Real-time operating systems (RTOS)  
   - Scheduling and timing constraints  
   - Hardware-software co-design  

3. Internet of Things (IoT) in CPS (20%)  
   - IoT device communication protocols (MQTT, REST, CoAP, Zigbee)  
   - Device identification and data transmission  
   - Edge computing in IoT deployments  
   - Scalability and network reliability  

4. Cybersecurity in CPS Environments  
   - Threat modeling for physical-cyber interfaces  
   - Vulnerability analysis in sensor and actuator networks  
   - Secure communication protocols (TLS, DTLS)  
   - Authentication and key management (PKI, mutual authentication)  
   - Intrusion detection in real-time systems

6. Fault Tolerance and Resilience

7. Case Studies in Smart Manufacturing

   - Role of learning factories in hands-on training  
   - Interdisciplinary skill development  

8. Human Factors and Operational Safety  
   - Operator interaction with CPS  
   - Decision-making under cyber threats  
   - Training and cognitive load in CPS environments  

9. Future Trends and Emerging Technologies  
   - AI-driven CPS decision-making  
   - Digital twins and simulation in CPS
