1. Competitive Market Analysis (20%) 
   - Market structure: perfect competition, oligopoly, monopoly
   - Porter’s Five Forces
   - SWOT analysis
   - Competitive positioning: value proposition, differentiation
   - Industry trends and disruption: digitalization, automation

2. Competitive Intelligence (CI) Collection and Analysis (20%)
   - Primary vs. secondary data sources
   - Patent databases: USPTO, WIPO, Google Patents
   - Financial disclosures: 10-K, earnings calls
   - Social media and online reviews
   - Reverse engineering of products and services

3. Intellectual Property (IP) and Competitive Advantage (20%)
   - Types of IP: patents, trademarks, copyrights, trade secrets
   - Patentability criteria: novelty, non-obviousness, utility
   - IP lifecycle: filing, maintenance, enforcement
   - IP portfolio strategy and risk management
   - IP due diligence in mergers and acquisitions and new product development

4. IP Analysis and Competitive Benchmarking
   - Systematic patent analysis: mapping, clustering, gap analysis
   - Patent landscaping and competitive mapping
   - Patent families and technological domains
   - Use of AI tools such as ChatGPT in patent analysis

5. IP Strategy and Innovation Management
   - Innovation pipelines and IP alignment
   - Open innovation and IP licensing
   - IP protection in emerging technologies: artificial intelligence, biotechnology
   - Ethical considerations in IP use and sharing

6. Case-Based and Experiential Learning in Competitive Analysis
   - Real-world case studies: Tesla, Apple, Siemens
   - Business simulation games: supply chain, market entry
   - Hands-on IP analysis projects: patent review of a product

7. Ethical and Legal Dimensions of Competitive Analysis
   - Compliance with anti-competitive laws: antitrust
   - Ethical use of data and intellectual property
   - Conflict of interest and confidentiality

8. Integration with Supply Chain and Operations
   - How supply chain decisions affect competitive positioning
   - Impact of operational efficiency on IP-driven innovation
