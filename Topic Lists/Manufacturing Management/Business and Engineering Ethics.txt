1. Foundational Principles of Engineering and Business Ethics (20%)
   - Definition and scope of ethics in engineering and business  
   - Core ethical frameworks (utilitarianism, deontology, virtue ethics)  
   - Ethical decision-making models (ethical dilemma analysis)  
   - Role of professional codes of conduct (ASCE, IEEE, ISO)  

2. Ethical Decision-Making in Engineering Design and Development  (20%)
   - Identifying stakeholders and their interests  
   - Risk assessment and mitigation in design phases  
   - Balancing innovation with safety and sustainability  
   - Case studies: design failures due to ethical lapses (Therac-25)  

3. Ethics in Supply Chain and Operations Management  (20%)
   - Fair labor practices and human rights in supply chains  
   - Environmental sustainability and ESG compliance  
   - Transparency and traceability in sourcing  
   - Ethical procurement and vendor selection  

4. Ethical Implications of Automation and AI in Manufacturing  
   - Bias in algorithmic decision-making  
   - Job displacement and workforce equity  
   - Data privacy and intellectual property in AI systems  
   - Accountability in autonomous systems  

5. Ethical Leadership and Organizational Culture  
   - Leadership responsibilities in ethical governance  
   - Building ethical organizational cultures  
   - Whistleblowing and internal reporting mechanisms  
   - Power dynamics and ethical accountability  

6. Ethical Issues in Intellectual Property and Innovation  
   - Patent misuse and infringement  
   - Open-source vs. proprietary innovation  
   - Ethical use of data in R&D  
   - Systematic patent analysis for ethical innovation  

7. Ethics in Quality Management and Continuous Improvement  
   - Integrity in quality assurance processes  
   - Avoiding falsification of data  
   - Ethical use of statistical process control (SPC)  
   - Responsibility in process optimization  

8. Ethical Case Studies and Real-World Applications  
   - Industry-specific case studies (automotive, aerospace)  
   - Simulation-based ethical scenarios (business games)  
   - Group debriefs and reflective analysis
