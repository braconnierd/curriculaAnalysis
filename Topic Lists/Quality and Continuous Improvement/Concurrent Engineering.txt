1. Product Lifecycle Integration  
   - Definition and scope of concurrent engineering  
   - Alignment of design, manufacturing, and operations phases  
   - Cross-functional team structure and roles  
   - Lifecycle cost analysis and trade-offs  

2. Systematic Design Process  
   - Requirements gathering and validation  
   - Concept generation and evaluation  
   - Design for manufacturability (DFM) and assembly  
   - Design for maintenance and sustainability  

3. Process Design and Optimization  
   - Process flowcharting and value stream mapping  
   - Identification of critical path and bottlenecks  
   - Tooling and fixture design (jigs, templates)  
   - Iterative prototyping and testing  

4. Statistical Process Control (SPC)  
   - X-bar and R charts construction and interpretation  
   - Process stability and capability (Cp, Cpk)  
   - Measurement system analysis (MSA)  
   - Detection of random vs. non-random variation  

5. Quality Management Systems (QMS)  
   - ISO 9001 principles and application  
   - Quality function deployment (QFD) and house of quality  
   - Failure mode and effects analysis (FMEA)  
   - Root cause analysis and corrective actions  

6. Integration of Digital Tools  
   - Use of CAD/CAM for design and simulation  
   - Digital twins and virtual prototyping  
   - Data collection and real-time monitoring in manufacturing  

7. Lean Manufacturing Principles  
   - Value stream analysis and waste elimination  
   - 5S methodology and workplace organization  
   - Just-in-time (JIT) production and pull systems  

8. Team-Based Learning and Problem Solving  
   - Project-based learning (PBL) with real-world case studies  
   - Role assignment and team dynamics  
   - Iterative feedback loops and PDCA cycle  

9. Evaluation and Continuous Improvement  
   - Performance metrics and KPIs  
   - Post-implementation review and lessons learned  
   - Feedback integration into future design cycles  

10. Ethical and Sustainable Engineering Practices  
   - Environmental impact assessment  
   - Lifecycle sustainability and circular design
