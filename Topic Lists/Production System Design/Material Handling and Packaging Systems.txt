1. Fundamentals of Material Handling Systems (30%)
   - Definition and classification of material handling systems  
   - Key performance indicators (KPIs): throughput, cycle time, energy consumption  
   - Types: conveyors, lifts, cranes, automated guided vehicles (AGVs)  
   - Selection criteria based on load, environment, and cost  

2. Packaging Design Principles (30%)
   - Functional requirements: protection, integrity, ease of use  
   - Material selection: sustainability, recyclability, mechanical properties  
   - Packaging structure: layers, seals, closures  
   - Regulatory compliance: food, pharmaceutical, hazardous materials  

3. Integration of Material Handling and Packaging in Manufacturing Flow  
   - Workflow design: sequence, bottlenecks, synchronization  
   - Interface design between handling and packaging stations  
   - Layout optimization using lean principles (5S, value stream mapping)  

4. Simulation-Based System Design  
   - Use of simulation tools (e.g., AnyLogic, FlexSim) for prototyping
   - Modeling material flow and packaging operations  
   - Scenario testing: capacity, failure, scalability  

5. Sustainability in Material Handling and Packaging  
   - Life cycle assessment (LCA) of materials and systems  
   - Energy efficiency and carbon footprint analysis  
   - Circular economy principles: reuse, recycling, minimal waste  

6. Additive Manufacturing Applications in Handling and Packaging  
   - Design of custom handling components using 3D printing  
   - Rapid prototyping of packaging structures  
   - Material limitations and environmental impact  

7. Maintenance Planning for Handling and Packaging Systems  
   - Preventive and predictive maintenance strategies  
   - Failure modes and effects analysis (FMEA)  
   - Maintenance scheduling using learning factory environments  

8. Human Factors and Ergonomics in Handling Systems  
   - Operator interaction with equipment  
   - Safety standards and risk mitigation  
   - Design for human usability and fatigue reduction  

9. Digital Twin Implementation in Material Handling Systems  
   - Real-time monitoring and feedback loops  
   - Data integration from sensors and IoT devices  
   - Use in predictive maintenance and performance optimization  

10. Case Studies in Industry 4.0 Environments  
    - Real-world applications in metal, food, and pharmaceutical industries  
    - Lessons learned from integrated product and manufacturing systems
