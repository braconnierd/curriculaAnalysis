1. Fundamentals of Process Planning (30%)
   - Definition and objectives of process planning  
   - Role of process planning in product lifecycle  
   - Types of processes: machining, forming, joining, assembly  
   - Selection criteria for process routes  

2. Process Planning Methodologies (30%)
   - Traditional vs. modern process planning approaches  
   - Flowcharting and process mapping techniques  
   - Decision trees for process selection  
   - Use of design for manufacturability (DFM) principles  

3. Computer-Aided Process Planning (CAPP) Systems  
   - Architecture of CAPP systems  
   - Input data: part geometry, materials, tolerances  
   - CAPP software modules: route generation, tool selection, operation sequencing  
   - Integration with CAD and CAM systems  
   - Case studies of CAPP implementation in real manufacturing   

4. Additive Manufacturing and Design Innovation 
   - Process planning for 3D printing (layer-by-layer, support structures)  
   - Design for additive manufacturing (DFAM) principles  
   - Material selection and print parameters in process planning  

5. Cross-Curricular Integration
   - Linking process planning with business and economics (cost, lead time)  
   - Simulation-based learning for real-world manufacturing scenarios  
   - Interdisciplinary project design (engineering + business)
