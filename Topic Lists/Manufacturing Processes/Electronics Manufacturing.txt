1. Foundations of Electronics Manufacturing  (20%)
   - History and evolution of electronics manufacturing  
   - Key milestones in semiconductor and circuit development  
   - Role of miniaturization and integration  
   - Materials science in electronics (metals, semiconductors, insulators)  

2. Design for Manufacturing (DFM) in Electronics  
   - DFM principles for PCBs and components  
   - Tolerancing, assembly constraints, and manufacturability  
   - Design for testability and repairability  

3. Electronics Assembly Processes  (20%)
   - Soldering techniques (wave, reflow, hand)  
   - Surface mount technology (SMT) and through-hole assembly  
   - Automated assembly lines and pick-and-place machines  
   - Quality control in assembly (visual, X-ray, AOI)  

4. Circuit and System Integration  (20%)
   - PCB design and layout (tools: Altium, KiCad, Eagle)  
   - Signal integrity and power distribution  
   - Interconnectivity and thermal management  

5. Additive Manufacturing in Electronics  
   - 3D printing of conductive materials and circuitry  
   - Applications in prototyping and custom components  
   - Material limitations and reliability  

6. Quality Assurance and Failure Analysis  
   - Defect identification (shorts, opens, delamination)  
   - Root cause analysis and failure mode modeling  

7. Sustainable and Green Manufacturing Practices  
   - E-waste reduction, energy efficiency, and lifecycle analysis  
   - Green design principles in electronics
