1. Process Design Fundamentals  (20%)
   - Definition and scope of process development  
   - Flowcharting and process mapping (value stream mapping)  
   - Inputs, outputs, and constraints in process design  
   - Design for manufacturability (DFM) and design for assembly (DFA)  
   - Iterative design cycles and feedback loops  

2. Fundamentals of Process Research  (20%)
   - Definition and scope of process research  
   - Research methodologies in engineering design (qualitative, quantitative, mixed methods)  
   - Role of process research in innovation cycles (design-to-production, feedback loops)  
   - Literature review and critical analysis of existing processes (gap identification and comparative evaluation)  

3. Design Thinking and Problem Framing (20%)
   - Identifying user needs and real-world problems through stakeholder interviews and observation  
   - Empathizing, defining, and ideating in design (mind mapping, brainstorming, and divergent thinking)  
   - Translating problems into actionable design challenges with measurable outcomes  
   - Validating design solutions through user testing and feedback iteration

4. Human-Centric Design in Industry 5.0  
   - User experience and human factors in process design (ergonomics, accessibility, cognitive load)  
   - Ethical considerations in automation and design (bias in AI, job displacement, transparency)  
   - Inclusive design principles (diversity, equity, accessibility in product and process design)  
   - Social and cultural implications of automated processes (human-machine collaboration, trust, accountability) 

5. Additive Manufacturing Process Development  
   - Material selection and layer-by-layer process logic  
   - Print parameters: temperature, speed, layer thickness  
   - Design for additive manufacturing (DfAM): lattice structures, overhangs, support generation  
   - Post-processing and dimensional accuracy  

6. Test Methodology and Validation  
   - Test planning: objectives, scope, resources  
   - Types of tests: functional, stress, durability, environmental  
   - Test data collection and instrumentation  
   - Failure analysis and root cause identification  

7. Evaluation and Performance Metrics  
   - KPIs for process efficiency and test reliability  
   - Benchmarking against industry standards  
   - Assessment of learning gains through test outcomes
