1. Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing (GD&T) 
   - Fundamentals of GD&T symbols and principles  
   - Feature control frames and datum references  
   - Tolerance stack-up and assembly fit analysis  
   - Application in machine element design

2. Engineering Drawing Standards and Practices  
   - ISO and ASME standards for technical drawings  
   - View selection, sectioning, and projection techniques  
   - Dimensioning conventions and callout formats  
   - Annotation, title blocks, and revision control 

3. CAD (Computer-Aided Design) Fundamentals (20%)
   - 2D sketching and constraint-based modeling  
   - 3D modeling: extrusion, revolution, sweeping  
   - Parametric modeling and feature trees  
   - File formats (STEP, IGES), interoperability  

4. CAM (Computer-Aided Manufacturing) Integration (20%)
   - Toolpath generation and machining operations  
   - CNC programming basics (G-code)  
   - Simulation and verification of toolpaths  
   - Material removal and cutting strategies   

5. CAE (Computer-Aided Engineering) Applications (20%) 
   - Finite element analysis (FEA) for stress and deformation  
   - Simulation of thermal and fluid dynamics  
   - Validation of design against performance criteria  

6. Product Lifecycle Management (PLM)  
   - Lifecycle phases: concept to end-of-life  
   - Data flow, digital twins, and traceability  
   - Integration of design, manufacturing, and sustainability 

7. Sustainable Manufacturing and Life Cycle Assessment (LCA)  
   - Environmental impact analysis (energy, emissions)  
   - Material selection and lifecycle cost modeling  
   - Sustainable design principles   

8. Manufacturing Process Analysis and Case Studies  
   - Unit process analysis and workflow mapping  
   - Case study integration in learning factory environments
