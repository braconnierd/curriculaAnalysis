1. Workforce Planning and Analysis  (20%)
   - Labor market trends and skill gaps  
   - Workforce forecasting methods (regression, scenario analysis)  
   - Competency mapping and job role breakdowns  
   - Organizational needs assessment  

2. Human Resource Management (HRM) in Workplaces  
   - Recruitment and selection processes  
   - Onboarding and orientation programs  
   - Performance appraisal and feedback systems  
   - Employee retention strategies  
   - Diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI) in hiring  

3. Labor Relations and Collective Bargaining (20%)  
   - Legal foundations of labor law (NLRA, labor contracts)  
   - Union formation and negotiation processes  
   - Conflict resolution and mediation techniques  
   - Workplace communication and grievance procedures  

4. Personnel Development and Training  
   - Needs assessment for training programs  
   - Designing and delivering training modules  
   - Skill development in technical and soft skills  
   - E-learning and blended learning platforms  

5. Management of Workforce Operations (20%)
   - Shift scheduling and workload distribution  
   - Supervisory leadership and team dynamics  
   - Safety and compliance management  
   - Workforce motivation and engagement  

6. Ethics and Governance in Workforce Practices  
   - Ethical decision-making in personnel decisions  
   - Fair labor practices and anti-discrimination policies  
   - Accountability and transparency in HR operations  

7. Performance Management and Continuous Improvement  
   - KPIs and performance metrics  
   - Feedback loops and improvement cycles (PDCA)  
   - Quality management integration (ISO 9001)  

8. Technology and Workforce Integration  
   - Digital tools for HR and labor management  
   - AI-driven workforce analytics  
   - Automation and its impact on job roles  

9. Case-Based and Experiential Learning  
   - Real-world case studies in labor disputes and HR decisions  
   - Simulation games for labor relations and workforce planning  

10. Policy and Regulatory Compliance  
   - Compliance with labor laws and regulations  
   - Risk assessment in workforce operations
