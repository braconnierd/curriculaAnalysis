1. Materials Science in Ceramics (15%)
   - Ceramic composition and phase diagrams  
   - Silica, alumina, and oxide chemistry  
   - Microstructure-property relationships  
   - Thermal expansion and brittleness  
   - Porosity and density measurement  
   - Environmental degradation and stability  

2. Ceramic Processing Techniques (15%)
   - Powder production (dry/wet milling, sieving)  
   - Pelletizing and shaping (pressing, extrusion, slip casting)  
   - Green body handling and defects  
   - Drying and shrinkage control  
   - Firing cycles (sintering, reduction, oxidation)  
   - Temperature profiles and thermal gradients  

3. Sintering and Density Optimization (15%)
   - Diffusion mechanisms in ceramics  
   - Sintering kinetics and activation energy  
   - Porosity reduction and grain growth  
   - Microstructural evolution during firing  

4. Ceramic Design and Form Development (15%)
   - Functional design (structural, aesthetic, thermal)  
   - Design for manufacturability (DFM)  
   - Tooling and mold design  
   - Surface finishing and texture  

5. Quality Control and Testing  
   - Mechanical testing (compressive strength, flexural strength)  
   - Dimensional accuracy and tolerancing  
   - Failure analysis and fracture mechanics  
   - Non-destructive testing (X-ray, ultrasonic)  

6. Sustainable and Green Ceramics 
   - Energy efficiency in firing  
   - Waste reduction and recycling  
   - Low-impact raw materials  
   - Life cycle assessment (LCA)  

7. Digital Tools and Simulation**  
   - CAD modeling of ceramic parts  
   - CAE simulation of sintering and stress  
   - Digital twin applications in ceramic processing  

8. Hands-on Laboratory and Failure Analysis  
   - Lab safety and equipment operation  
   - Experimental design and iterative prototyping  
   - Failure investigation and root cause analysis  

9. Industry Integration and Case Studies  
   - Real-world applications (tiles, bioceramics, aerospace)  
   - Industry 4.0 integration (IoT, sensors in kilns)  
   - Academic-industry collaboration models
