1. Introduction to Statistical Process Control (SPC)

2. Process Design and Measurement Planning  
   - Specification development  
   - Critical dimensions and edge quality  
   - Selection of measurement points (top side, diagonals)  
   - Consistent layout and orientation (numbered side)  
   - Sampling strategy

   - Principles of X-bar and R charts  
   - Sample size selection  
   - Calculation of UCL, CL, LCL

   - Detection of random vs. non-random variation  
   - Capability indices (Cp, Cpk)

5. Variation Sources and Root Cause Analysis

   - Feedback loops from trial runs
