1. Statistical Process Control (SPC) (20%)
   - Control charts: X-bar, R
   - Process stability
   - Capability indices: Cp, Cpk
   - Sources of variation: random, non-random
   - Data collection protocols
   - Interpretation of control charts

2. Quality Management Systems (QMS) (20%)
   - ISO 9001 standards
   - Quality planning
   - Quality assurance
   - Internal and external audits
   - Continual improvement: PDCA cycle
   - Root cause analysis: fishbone diagrams
   - Documentation requirements

3. Manufacturing Regulations and Compliance (20%)
   - OSHA safety standards and workplace regulations
   - EPA environmental regulations: emissions, waste disposal
   - CE marking requirements
   - Product safety standards: flammability, electrical safety
   - Environmental impact assessments
   - Compliance documentation: records, logs, certificates
   - Audit readiness procedures
   - Regulatory updates and monitoring

4. Ethics in Manufacturing Operations
   - Responsible innovation in product and process development
   - Safety ethics and worker well-being
   - Environmental stewardship and sustainability
   - Intellectual property rights management
   - Whistleblower protection policies
   - Ethical decision-making frameworks
   - Conflict of interest management

5. Supply Chain Financial Impact and Risk Management
   - Cost of non-compliance with regulations and standards
   - Inventory cost modeling and optimization
   - Supply chain risk assessment: supplier failure, disruptions
   - Financial performance metrics: ROI, cost of quality, throughput efficiency
   - Resilience planning
   - Integration of compliance costs into financial planning

6. Design for Manufacturing (DFM) and Process Integration
   - Product design principles for manufacturability
   - Manufacturability assessments: tolerances, assembly complexity
   - Process flow design: assembly, inspection, packaging
   - Integration of design and production operations
   - Quality-by-design principles
   - Quality Function Deployment methodology
   - Digital tools in DFM: CAD, simulation

7. Hands-On Learning and Experiential Training
   - Business simulation games: supply chain, inventory, quality control
   - Learning factory environments: real-time production setups
   - SAP-based logistics and operations labs
   - Real-world process execution: mock production lines
   - Feedback loops and performance evaluation
   - Reflective practice in experiential learning

8. Continuous Quality Improvement (CQI)
   - Lean manufacturing principles: value stream mapping, 5S
   - Kaizen initiatives and implementation
   - Six Sigma methodologies: DMAIC
   - Problem-solving methodologies: 5 Whys, PDCA
   - Team-based improvement cycles
   - Performance metrics tracking
   - Root cause analysis in operational settings

9. Project Management in Manufacturing
   - Project lifecycle management: initiation, planning, execution, monitoring, closure
   - Scheduling and timeline development: Gantt charts
   - Resource allocation and budgeting
   - Risk identification and mitigation
   - Stakeholder communication strategies
   - Scope control
   - Integration of quality and compliance into project planning

10. Interdisciplinary Engineering Education and Industry Collaboration
   - Industry-university partnerships and co-developed curricula
   - Scalable learning environments: modular labs, virtual simulations
   - Integration of real-world case studies into coursework
   - Student engagement with industry professionals
   - Curriculum alignment with industry standards and emerging technologies: Industry 4.0, smart manufacturing
   - Evaluation of educational outcomes against industry benchmarks
