1. Market Analysis (20%)
   - Market segmentation (demographic, geographic, behavioral)  
   - Market sizing and forecasting techniques  
   - Competitive landscape analysis (5 Forces, SWOT)  
   - Market entry strategies and barriers  
   - Consumer behavior and demand drivers  

2. Sales Strategy and Execution  (20%)  
   - Sales process modeling (lead to close)  
   - Sales forecasting and pipeline management  
   - Sales enablement tools (CRM, sales playbooks)  
   - Pricing strategies (value-based, penetration, skimming)  
   - Sales performance metrics (conversion rate, average deal size)  

3. Product Lifecycle Management (PLM)  (20%)
   - Lifecycle phases (conception, introduction, growth, maturity, decline)  
   - Lifecycle cost analysis  
   - Design for lifecycle (durability, repairability, recyclability)  
   - Lifecycle data integration and digital twins  
   - End-of-life management and sustainability  

4. Life Cycle Assessment (LCA)  
   - LCA phases (goal definition, inventory, impact assessment, interpretation)  
   - Environmental impact metrics (carbon footprint, water use)  
   - LCA in product design and manufacturing decisions  
   - LCA software tools and data sources  

5. Sustainable Manufacturing and Value Creation  
   - Sustainable design principles  
   - Circular economy models  
   - Waste reduction and resource efficiency  
   - Life cycle cost vs. environmental cost trade-offs  

6. New Product Development (NPD) and Entrepreneurial Thinking  
   - NPD process (idea generation, concept development, testing)  
   - Prototyping and validation methods  
   - Risk assessment and innovation management  
   - Entrepreneurial mindset and decision-making  

7. Case Study Integration 
   - Real-world industry case studies (market failure, successful lifecycle transition)  
   - Critical analysis and solution design  
   - Group-based problem solving and presentation  

8. Digital Tools and Simulation
   - Use of VR, simulation, and digital twins in sales and lifecycle planning  
   - Digital platforms for market and sales data visualization
